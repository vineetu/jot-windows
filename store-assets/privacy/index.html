Jot for Windows — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

"By default, Jot for Windows transcribes your speech entirely on your device. Nothing about your voice or your transcripts leaves your PC unless you deliberately turn on an optional cloud AI feature and supply your own API key."

Data collection

No accounts, no analytics, no telemetry

"Jot for Windows does not require an account, and does not collect or transmit usage analytics, crash reports, or personal information. There are no servers of ours that receive your usage data. An on-device activity log is kept for troubleshooting, but it stays in your chosen data folder and is never sent anywhere."

Microphone

Used only while you are dictating

"Jot accesses your microphone only while you are actively dictating — that is, while you hold or toggle the dictation hotkey. The captured audio is transcribed to text on your device and is never uploaded, streamed, or stored on any server. Windows privacy settings also gate microphone access at the system level."

Speech processing

On-device by default

"All speech recognition runs locally using on-device ONNX speech models (Parakeet-TDT or Nemotron), downloaded once from Hugging Face the first time you run the app. During normal dictation, your voice recordings and transcripts stay on your machine — they are not sent to any server."

Network

Only when you ask for it

"Jot for Windows makes a network request in exactly these cases: (1) downloading the speech model on first run; (2) if you import an audio or video file, a one-time download of the FFmpeg media decoder used to read that file; (3) if you enable the optional AI cleanup or rewrite feature and configure a cloud provider (Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic) with your own API key, your transcript text is sent to that provider to process your request — or to a local Ollama server you run yourself, which never leaves your machine; (4) if you choose to submit in-app feedback, which sends only your message, the app version, and platform; and (5) an optional read of a public, no-login donations summary. If you never turn on a cloud AI provider, your transcript content never leaves your PC."

Third parties

No trackers, no ad SDKs

"We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services — there are no SDKs collecting data on your behavior. If you opt in to a cloud AI provider, your transcript text is sent directly to that provider under their own terms and privacy policy; we do not proxy, log, or store it ourselves. You are responsible for the API key you supply and your account with that provider."

Children's privacy

Not directed at children

"Jot for Windows is a general-purpose productivity tool and is not directed at children. It collects no personal information from anyone, including children."

Contact

Questions?

"If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us via the GitHub repository."
